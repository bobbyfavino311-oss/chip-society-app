❄️ Frozen Neon — Chip Society Table Theme Prompt

Theme Name: Frozen Neon

Design Goal:
Create a premium futuristic poker table theme inspired by ice, glass, luxury technology, and neon lighting. The theme should feel like a high-end casino inside an Apple-designed arctic resort. Minimalist, elegant, and highly readable. Avoid cartoon elements, snowflakes, winter scenery, or anything gimmicky.

⸻

Background

Use a deep midnight blue to near-black gradient background.

The background should feature:

* Massive translucent frosted-glass circles
* Soft overlapping icy blue shapes
* Blurred glass-like gradients
* Subtle depth and layering
* Very low visual noise
* No realistic environment
* No table felt texture

The effect should feel like looking through premium frozen glass illuminated by neon lighting.

⸻

Color Palette

Primary Colors:

* Ice Blue (#8FEFFF)
* Electric Cyan (#00D9FF)
* Arctic White (#F5FCFF)

Accent Colors:

* Frost Silver (#DDE8F0)
* Deep Navy (#08101E)

Glow Colors:

* Cyan glow
* White glow
* Subtle blue aura

No purple.
No pink.
No warm colors.

⸻

Table Elements

Community Card Tray:

* Frosted glass panel
* Thin cyan illuminated border
* Soft internal glow
* Rounded corners
* Glassmorphism styling

Pot Display:

* Floating glass capsule
* Bright cyan numbers
* White labels
* Premium casino appearance

Player Areas:

* Frosted glass circular platforms
* Soft cyan edge lighting
* Clean futuristic design

⸻

Lighting

* Soft bloom effects
* Delicate cyan rim lighting
* Frosted glass reflections
* Minimal neon accents
* Subtle volumetric glow

Everything should feel premium and expensive.

⸻

Visual Style

Inspired By:

* Apple Vision Pro
* Luxury yacht interiors
* High-end casino lounges
* Frosted glass UI design
* Modern electric vehicles
* Scandinavian luxury architecture

Keywords:

minimalist, futuristic, luxury, frozen glass, premium casino, glassmorphism, cyan glow, ice blue lighting, elegant, sophisticated, atmospheric, ultra-clean, high-end UI, modern poker environment

⸻

Important Constraints

* No snowflakes
* No ice crystals
* No mountains
* No winter scenery
* No cartoon effects
* No fantasy elements
* No clutter
* No busy patterns

Focus entirely on:
dark luxury + frozen glass + cyan neon + premium poker atmosphere.
