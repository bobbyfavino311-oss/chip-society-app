DEAL LOGIC

When player taps DEAL:

1. Deduct Ante.
2. Deduct optional Tie Bet if placed.
3. Deal one face-up card to dealer.
4. Deal one face-up card to player.
5. Compare values.
6. Resolve outcome.

Dealer and player cards must appear immediately with correct rank and suit.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BASIC RESULT RULES

If player card is higher:

Ante wins 1:1.

Tie Bet loses unless first cards match.

If dealer card is higher:

Ante loses.

Tie Bet loses unless first cards match.

If cards match:

Trigger WAR decision:

• Surrender
• Go To War

Do not immediately mark win or loss on first-card tie.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TIE BET RULE

Tie Bet only wins if the first dealer card and first player card match in rank.

Tie Bet pays:

10:1

If first cards do not match:

Tie Bet loses.

The UI must clearly show:

Tie Bet -150K

or

Tie Bet +1.5M

depending on outcome.
