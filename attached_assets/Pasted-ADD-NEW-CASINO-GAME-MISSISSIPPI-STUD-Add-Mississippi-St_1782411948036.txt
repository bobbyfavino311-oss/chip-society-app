GAME CONCEPT

Mississippi Stud is a community-card poker game where the player is not playing against the dealer.

Instead, the player is trying to make the strongest possible five-card poker hand using:

• 2 Hole Cards
• 3 Community Cards

The player wins by making Pair of Jacks or Better according to the Mississippi Stud pay table.

There is no dealer qualification.

The dealer simply deals the cards and resolves the wagers.
