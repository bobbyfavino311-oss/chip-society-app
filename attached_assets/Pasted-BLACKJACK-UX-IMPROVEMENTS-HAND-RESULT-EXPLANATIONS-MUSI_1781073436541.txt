BLACKJACK UX IMPROVEMENTS — HAND RESULT EXPLANATIONS + MUSIC SYSTEM

The current Blackjack implementation correctly determines wins, losses, pushes, and split-hand outcomes, but it does not clearly communicate WHY the result occurred.

The player should never have to guess why they won, lost, pushed, or partially won a split hand.

Every hand result must include a short, easy-to-read explanation.
