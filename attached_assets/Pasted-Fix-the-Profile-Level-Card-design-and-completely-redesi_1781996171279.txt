FORTUNE COOKIE SYSTEM REDESIGN

CURRENT PROBLEM

The Fortune Cookie reward economy is completely unbalanced.

Examples:

* Common cookies awarding rewards that belong in higher tiers.
* Low-tier rewards occasionally exceeding high-tier rewards.
* Cookie rarity does not properly reflect prize value.

This damages:

* Reward progression
* Excitement
* Fairness
* Player trust
* Long-term retention

Higher rarity cookies must always provide higher value rewards.
