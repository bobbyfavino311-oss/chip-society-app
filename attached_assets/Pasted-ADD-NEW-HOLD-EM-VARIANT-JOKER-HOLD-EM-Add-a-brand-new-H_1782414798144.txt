DESIGN GOAL

Joker Hold’em should feel like a premium, exciting variation of Texas Hold’em while remaining instantly familiar to existing players.

The only gameplay differences should be:

• Two Jokers are added to the deck.
• Jokers are always wild.
• The engine automatically selects the strongest possible hand.
• Five of a Kind becomes the highest-ranking hand in the game.

Everything else—including betting, gameplay flow, matchmaking, UI, and controls—should remain identical to standard Texas Hold’em, ensuring players can jump in immediately without learning a completely new game.
