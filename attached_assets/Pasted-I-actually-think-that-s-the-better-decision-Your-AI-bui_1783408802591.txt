The finished result should feel modern, premium, and effortless to browse. Players should be able to swipe through the casino games in just a few seconds, immediately recognize each game, and start playing without navigating through a long scrolling list.

I would make one additional improvement: mirror the poker navigation. Since you’re simplifying the Play tab, both Poker and Casino should follow the same mental model:

* Poker → Swipe between Hold’em, Short Deck, Omaha, Joker → Choose AI or Live → Choose stakes.
* Casino → Swipe between Three Card, Blackjack, Ultimate, etc. → Press Play → Choose table limits.

That consistency will make the entire Play section feel much more polished and intuitive.
