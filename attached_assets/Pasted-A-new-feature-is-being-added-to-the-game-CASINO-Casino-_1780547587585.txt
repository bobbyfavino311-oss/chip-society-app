THREE CARD POKER RULES

Use official Three Card Poker rules.

Only a standard 52-card deck.

No Jokers.

Player competes directly against the dealer.

Each participant receives three cards.

Dealer receives three cards.

Dealer hand remains hidden until player action is complete.

===========================================================

BETTING OPTIONS

ANTE

Player bets against the dealer.

To win, the player's final hand must beat the dealer's hand.

PLAY WAGER

After seeing their cards, the player may continue.

The Play wager must exactly match the Ante wager.

PAIR PLUS

Optional side bet based only on the player's hand.

Dealer hand does not matter.

ANTE BONUS

Automatically awarded when qualifying hands are achieved.

===========================================================

PLAYER DECISIONS

After receiving three cards:

PLAY

Continue by matching the Ante bet.

or

FOLD

Forfeit the Ante and end the hand.

===========================================================

DEALER QUALIFICATION

Dealer must have:

QUEEN HIGH OR BETTER

to qualify.

If dealer does NOT qualify:

Ante bet wins.

Play wager pushes.

Ante Bonus still pays if qualified.

HAND RESOLUTION

If dealer qualifies and beats player:

Ante loses.

Play loses.

Ante pays 1:1

Play pays 1:1

No loss.
