FORTUNE COOKIE 🥠 AUDIO SYSTEM

The Fortune Cookie feature should not feel like a simple reward popup.

Opening a cookie should feel like a premium casino event with satisfying audio feedback from start to finish.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

COOKIE OPEN SOUND

When the player taps OPEN COOKIE:

Play a realistic fortune cookie cracking sound.

Sound characteristics:

• Crisp cookie snap
• Crunching shell sound
• Light cracking texture
• Satisfying ASMR quality
• Short and clean

Avoid:

• Cartoon crack sounds
• Explosions
• Generic button clicks

The player should instantly feel like they physically cracked open a real fortune cookie.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FORTUNE REVEAL SOUND

After the cookie cracks:

The fortune slip slowly rises.

As it rises:

Play a subtle magical sound.

Examples:

• Soft chime
• Wind bell
• Gentle sparkle sound
• Rising shimmer effect

The audio should build anticipation.
