# DAILY REWARDS — FLOATING MINIMAL REDESIGN

The current Daily Rewards redesign feels too heavy.

The rounded square behind the icons makes the cards feel cluttered and introduces an unnecessary layer.

The goal is to make the Daily Rewards section feel effortless, premium, and seamless.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

REMOVE THE INNER BOXES

Delete the rounded square glass container behind both icons.

Do not replace it with another shape.

The icons should float directly on the card.

The card itself is the only container.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PROGRESS ARC

Keep the progress indicator, but refine it.

• Remove the full circular appearance.
• Use a thin 270° progress arc.
• Reduce thickness by approximately 30%.
• Make the inactive portion very subtle.
• Apply only a soft glow to the active portion.

The arc should frame the icon, not dominate it.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DAILY SPIN ICON

Replace the current target-like symbol.

Use a minimalist prize wheel icon.

Requirements:

• Clearly recognizable as a Wheel of Fortune.
• 8–10 wheel segments.
• Small center hub.
• Tiny indicator pointer at the top.
• Simple geometric design.
• Consistent line thickness with the rest of the app.

It should immediately communicate "spin."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STREAK ICON

Remove the trophy.

Replace it with a clean custom flame icon.

Requirements:

• Minimal design.
• Modern geometry.
• No emoji styling.
• No excessive detail.
• Slightly rounded edges.

The flame should immediately communicate momentum and consistency.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FLOATING EFFECT

The icons should appear suspended above the card.

Do NOT place them inside a visible badge.

Instead, create the illusion of depth using:

• Very subtle radial glow.
• Soft ambient lighting.
• Slight shadow beneath the icon.

There should be no visible circle or square behind the icon.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ICON SCALE

Increase icon size by approximately 15–20%.

Since the container is removed, the icons should occupy more visual space.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CARD MATERIAL

Keep the premium smoked-glass cards.

Maintain:

• Soft vertical gradient.
• Soft edge lighting.
• Very subtle border.
• Premium shadow.

Do not introduce additional containers or layers.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

VISUAL GOAL

The Daily Rewards cards should feel open, elegant, and lightweight.

The eye should see:

Progress Arc

↓

Floating Icon

↓

Label

↓

Timer

Nothing should feel boxed in or crowded.

The design should resemble a premium automotive dashboard or luxury watch interface rather than stacked UI components.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FINAL VALIDATION

✅ Replace the target-like icon with a proper prize wheel.
✅ Replace the trophy with a minimalist flame.
✅ Thin the progress arc.
