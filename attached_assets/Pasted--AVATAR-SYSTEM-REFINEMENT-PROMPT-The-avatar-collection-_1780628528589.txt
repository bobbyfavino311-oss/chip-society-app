### AVATAR SYSTEM REFINEMENT PROMPT

The avatar collection is moving in the right direction, but several issues still need to be corrected for consistency, premium presentation, and overall quality.

---

## 1. REMOVE WHITE BORDERS / LETTERBOXING

The following avatars currently contain white empty space around the artwork:

* Flamingo
* Vice Skyline
* Yacht

### Required Fix

The artwork must completely fill the circular avatar crop.

* No white side bars
* No white top bars
* No letterboxing
* No pillarboxing
* No empty space

The artwork should be intelligently scaled and cropped so the image fills the entire circular frame.

The avatar should feel like a premium profile picture, not a rectangular image placed inside a circle.

---

## 2. FLAMINGO AVATAR

Current Issues:

* White borders visible
* Artwork not filling circle

### Fix

* Scale artwork to full circular crop
* Flamingo should occupy approximately 60–70% of the frame
* Emphasize neon water reflections
* Improve contrast and vibrancy
* Remove any empty space

Goal:

A premium Miami resort flamingo standing in neon water at sunset.

Selected avatar should use:

* Slight glow around avatar ring

* Slightly brighter avatar border

Nothing else.

Keep the layout clean.
