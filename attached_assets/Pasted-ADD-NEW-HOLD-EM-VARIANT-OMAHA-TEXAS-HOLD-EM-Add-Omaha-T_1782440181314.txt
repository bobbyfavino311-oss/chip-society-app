GAME NAME

OMAHA TEXAS HOLD’EM

Subtitle:

4 hole cards • Use exactly 2

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CORE GAME RULES

Omaha follows the same betting structure as Texas Hold’em.

Betting rounds:

• Pre-Flop
• Flop
• Turn
• River
• Showdown

Community cards:

• 5 total community cards

Each player receives:

• 4 private hole cards

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

GOLDEN RULE

This is the most important Omaha rule.

Players must use:

Exactly 2 cards from their 4 hole cards

and

Exactly 3 cards from the 5 community cards

to make their final 5-card poker hand.

Do NOT allow:

• 0 hole cards
• 1 hole card
• 3 hole cards
• 4 hole cards
• 4 community cards
• 5 community cards

The hand evaluator must enforce this rule every time.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HAND RANKINGS

Use standard poker hand rankings:

• Royal Flush
• Straight Flush
• Four of a Kind
• Full House
• Flush
• Straight
• Three of a Kind
• Two Pair
• Pair
• High Card

No new hand rankings are added.

Do not use Joker Hold’em rules.

Do not use Short Deck rules.

Omaha uses standard 52-card deck poker rankings.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DECK

Use a standard 52-card deck.

No Jokers.

No removed cards.

No Short Deck structure.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

GAMEPLAY FLOW

Omaha should play exactly like the existing Hold’em engine except for the hole-card count and hand evaluation rule.

Flow:

1. Small blind posts.
2. Big blind posts.
3. Each player receives 4 hole cards.
4. Pre-Flop betting.
5. Flop reveals 3 community cards.
6. Flop betting.
7. Turn reveals 1 community card.
8. Turn betting.
9. River reveals 1 community card.
10. River betting.
11. Showdown.
12. Best Omaha-legal hand wins.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SHOWDOWN LOGIC

At showdown:

Evaluate every valid combination using:

• exactly 2 hole cards
• exactly 3 community cards

Return the strongest possible legal Omaha hand.

Example:

Player hole cards:

A♠ A♦ 7♣ 2♥

Board:

A♥ K♠ K♦ 9♣ 3♠

The player may only use two hole cards.

Valid hand:

A♠ A♦ + A♥ K♠ K♦

Full House

Do not accidentally use three hole cards.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

IMPORTANT HAND EVALUATION WARNING

Do not reuse standard Hold’em hand evaluation without modification.

Texas Hold’em lets players use any combination of hole cards and board cards.

Omaha does not.

Omaha must use exactly 2 hole cards and exactly 3 board cards.

This rule must be hardcoded into the Omaha evaluator.
