GAME CONCEPT

High Card Flush is a heads-up casino poker game where the player competes against the dealer.

Unlike traditional poker, hands are ranked by the number of suited cards (flush cards), not by standard poker hands.

The objective is to finish with a stronger flush than the dealer.

The player receives:

• 7 cards

Dealer receives:

• 7 cards

The player attempts to have:

• More cards of one suit than the dealer

If both have the same number of suited cards, compare:

Highest flush card

If tied:

Second highest

Then third

Continue until a winner is determined.

If every flush card matches exactly:

The hand is a Push.

Straight Flushes have NO special value when comparing the player’s hand against the dealer.
