# HEADER LOGO BACKDROP REPLACEMENT — NATIVE CENTERED GLOW HALO

Remove the current image-based background behind the Chip Society logo.

Do NOT replace it with another image.

The current image is difficult to center responsively and keeps appearing left-shifted. Replace it with a native layered gradient backdrop created entirely from CSS, SVG, or platform-native shapes.

This backdrop must remain perfectly centered behind the Chip Society logo on every screen size.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

# DESIGN GOAL

Create a soft, layered, atmospheric backdrop behind the Chip Society logo that preserves the same energy as the current blue-purple oval effect.

It should feel:

• cinematic
• premium
• deep
• softly illuminated
• centered
• seamless

It must NOT look like:

• a hard circle
• a visible oval card
• a solid panel
• a bubble
• an image
• a spotlight with obvious edges

The effect should look like ambient blue-purple light suspended behind the logo.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

# USE NATIVE GRADIENT LAYERS

Build the backdrop from exactly three centered gradient layers.

LAYER 1 — LARGE NAVY HALO

• Wide horizontal ellipse
• Width: 86% of screen
• Height: 280–320px
• Center X: exactly 50% of viewport
• Center Y: aligned with the vertical center of the Chip Society logo
• Dark navy-blue
• Low opacity
• Very soft feathered edges

LAYER 2 — INNER CYAN-BLUE GLOW

• Width: approximately 68% of screen
• Height: approximately 220–250px
• Same exact center point as Layer 1
• Deep cyan-blue tint
• Lower opacity than the outer halo
• Strong blur
• No hard edge

LAYER 3 — PURPLE AMBIENT WASH

• Width: approximately 76% of screen
• Height: approximately 260–290px
• Same exact center point
• Dark violet-purple tint
• Very low opacity
• Blended softly into the other two layers

All three layers must share one identical center point.

Do not offset any layer left or right.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

# POSITIONING — HARD REQUIREMENT

Position the backdrop using the actual viewport center.

Use:

left: 50%
transform: translateX(-50%)

or the platform-equivalent centered alignment.

Do NOT use:

• fixed left pixel offsets
• manual margins
• absolute positioning based on the music button
• centering between the music and notification buttons
• percentage guesses such as 46%, 48%, or 52%

The center must remain exactly 50% of the screen width.

The Chip Society logo and the backdrop must share the same center axis.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

# SHAPE

The backdrop should be a wide ellipse, not a perfect circle.

Recommended proportion:

width approximately 1.8–2.1 times the height

The shape should frame the full wordmark comfortably.

Do not allow it to touch the left or right screen edges.

Do not create a visible perimeter.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

# EDGE FALLOFF

Use strong feathering and blur so the backdrop dissolves naturally into the dark Home screen background.

There must be:

• no visible border
• no rectangle
• no cut line
• no obvious mask
• no sharp oval edge

The glow should become fully transparent before reaching Daily Rewards.
