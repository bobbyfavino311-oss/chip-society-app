Frozen Neon Theme — Revision Prompt

The Frozen Neon theme is close, but it currently looks like a wallpaper panel placed on top of a dark background rather than a true full-screen environment.

The most noticeable issue is the dark blue vertical strip on the right side of the screen. This breaks immersion and makes the theme feel unfinished. The background must extend seamlessly edge-to-edge across the entire display with no visible borders, panels, rectangles, containers, or hard transitions.

⸻

Required Changes

Full-Screen Environment

The Frozen Neon theme must become a complete screen environment.

Remove:

* Right-side dark blue strip
* Any visible rectangular background panel
* Any hard edges
* Any visible theme boundaries
* Any feeling that the theme is a layer sitting on top of another background

Replace with:

* One continuous full-screen composition
* Seamless gradients extending to all screen edges
* Background elements flowing naturally across the entire display

The player should never be able to identify where the background begins or ends.

⸻

Background Design

Use:

* Deep navy-to-black gradients
* Large translucent frosted-glass circles
* Massive overlapping icy-blue glass forms
* Soft cyan atmospheric glow
* Smooth layered depth

The background should feel like:

* Looking through luxury frozen glass
* Apple Vision Pro marketing imagery
* High-end futuristic architecture
* Premium electric vehicle interior lighting

Everything should blend together naturally.

⸻

Shape Improvements

Current circles feel disconnected.

Update them to:

* Extend beyond screen edges
* Overlap naturally
* Vary greatly in scale
* Fade softly into each other
* Create a sense of depth

Think:

Giant glass structures floating behind the UI rather than decorative circles.

⸻

Lighting Improvements

Add:

* Soft cyan bloom
* Subtle glass reflections
* Frosted highlights
* Gentle volumetric glow

Do NOT add:

* Lens flares
* Bright neon streaks
* Excessive glow
* Busy visual effects

The theme should remain elegant and minimal.

⸻

Community Card Tray

Keep the existing tray design but improve it.

Requirements:

* Frosted glass appearance
* More depth
* Stronger edge lighting
* Subtle internal reflections
* Premium luxury finish

The tray should feel like it was machined from illuminated crystal glass.

⸻

Pot Display

Keep centered.

Enhancements:

* Slightly thicker glass effect
* Soft cyan glow
* More premium typography
* Greater contrast against background

⸻

Design Philosophy

This theme should not look like a casino game background.

It should look like:

* Apple designed a poker app
* Porsche designed a poker app
* Vision Pro meets luxury poker

Keywords:

ultra-premium, frozen glass, cyan neon, glassmorphism, luxury technology, minimalist, futuristic, elegant, seamless full-screen environment, atmospheric depth, Apple-level design quality

⸻

Critical Instruction

The entire screen must feel like one continuous frozen-neon world. There can be no visible panels, borders, strips, boxes, or background sections. Everything must blend seamlessly edge-to-edge across the display.
