FORTUNE COOKIE — OPEN ALL + PROFILE STAT UPDATE

Add an Open All Cookies feature to the Fortune Cookie section, similar to the Scratch All feature on Lottery Tickets.

⸻

OPEN ALL COOKIES BUTTON

Add a button near the existing Open Cookie button:

OPEN ALL

This button opens all cookies of the currently selected rarity.

Example:

Selected tab:

Legendary

Player owns:

Legendary x9

Pressing:

OPEN ALL

should open all 9 Legendary Cookies at once.

⸻

OPEN ALL BEHAVIOR

Open All must respect the selected cookie rarity.

If selected:

Common → opens all Common Cookies

Rare → opens all Rare Cookies

Epic → opens all Epic Cookies

Legendary → opens all Legendary Cookies

Mythic → opens all Mythic Cookies if owned

Do not default back to Common.

Do not open the wrong rarity.

⸻

BULK REWARD SUMMARY

After Open All completes, show a summary screen.

Example:

9 Legendary Cookies Opened

Rewards:

• Chips: +8.4M
• XP: +25K
• Tickets: +12

Button:

COLLECT

Rewards should be added immediately and saved server-side.

⸻

ANIMATION

Do not play the full cookie crack animation for every cookie.

Instead:

• Play one fast premium opening animation
• Show multiple cookie cracks quickly
• Reveal total rewards at once

This prevents the feature from feeling slow.

⸻

ZERO COOKIE STATE

If player owns 0 cookies of selected rarity:

Disable Open All.

Show:

NO LEGENDARY COOKIES

or the selected rarity name.

⸻

COOKIES OPENED STAT

Add a new stat to the Player Profile:

Cookies Opened

This stat tracks every Fortune Cookie opened.

It should include cookies opened by:

• Open Cookie
• Open All
• Any future cookie opening method

⸻

STAT COUNTING RULE

If player opens:

1 cookie manually

Cookies Opened +1

If player uses Open All on:

9 cookies

Cookies Opened +9

If player opens:

2 Mythic Cookies

Cookies Opened +2

⸻

PROFILE LOCATIONS

Display Cookies Opened on:

• Player Profile
• Social Profile
• Stats section
• Any future leaderboard/stat page

⸻

SERVER REQUIREMENT

Cookies Opened must be saved server-side.

It must persist after:

• Logout
• App restart
• Device switch
• Server refresh

Do not store this only locally.

⸻

FINAL VALIDATION

Verify:

✅ Open All opens the selected rarity only
✅ Rewards are totaled correctly
✅ Cookie inventory decreases correctly
✅ Rewards are saved server-side
✅ Cookies Opened increases by the exact number opened
✅ Profile displays Cookies Opened
✅ Social profile displays Cookies Opened
✅ Open All does not reset to Common
✅ Open All works for Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic if owned
