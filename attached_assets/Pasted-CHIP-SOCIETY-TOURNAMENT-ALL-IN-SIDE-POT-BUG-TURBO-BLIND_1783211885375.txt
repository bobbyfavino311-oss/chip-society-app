1. ALL-IN CHIP DISTRIBUTION BUG (CRITICAL)

There is a major bug in tournament gameplay that also previously existed in regular gameplay.

Current example:

Player Stack:

200,000

AI Stack:

18,000

Player goes All-In.

AI calls.

Player loses.

Current result:

The AI incorrectly receives the player’s entire 200,000 chip stack.

This is incorrect.

⸻

Correct Poker Rule

A player can only win the amount they have matched.

In this example:

Player:

200,000

AI:

18,000

Maximum pot between those two players is:

18,000 (AI)

18,000 (Player)

Total Main Pot:

36,000

The remaining:

182,000

Must immediately be returned to the player.

The AI can NEVER win chips it never matched.

⸻

Correct Result

Player loses.

AI receives:

36,000 total pot

Player keeps:

182,000

Player’s remaining stack:

182,000

NOT zero.

⸻

Required Fix

Audit the betting engine.

Specifically inspect:

* All-in logic
* Main pot calculation
* Side pot calculation
* Tournament betting engine
* Regular gameplay betting engine

Ensure both systems use the exact same betting rules.

⸻

Side Pot Rules

Whenever stacks differ:

Always create:

Main Pot

Side Pot(s)

Only players contributing to each pot may win that pot.

Unmatched chips must immediately return to their owner.

Never transfer unmatched chips to another player.

2. TURBO BLIND STRUCTURE BUG

Turbo tournaments are currently increasing blinds incorrectly.

Current behavior:

Blinds only increase when a player is eliminated.

This is incorrect.

⸻

Required Behavior

Turbo tournaments increase blinds based on completed hands.

NOT eliminations.

NOT elapsed time.

NOT player count.

⸻

Turbo Rule

Increase blinds:

Every 3 completed hands

Examples:

Hand 1

Hand 2

Hand 3

⬆ Blind Increase

Hand 4

Hand 5

Hand 6

⬆ Blind Increase

Continue for the entire tournament.

⸻

Blind Counter

Track:

Completed Hands

Every time a hand fully finishes:

Increment:

HandsPlayed++

When:

HandsPlayed % 3 == 0

Increase blinds.

Reset countdown.

⸻

Tournament Banner

After every third hand display:

BLINDS INCREASED

Small Blind:

100 → 200

Big Blind:

200 → 400

Display briefly.

Fade out.

⸻

Applies To

Every Turbo tournament.

Examples:

Turbo Heat

Turbo Championship

Turbo Sit & Go

Short Deck Turbo

Joker Turbo

Future Turbo events

All Turbo tournaments use the same blind progression rule.

⸻

Non-Turbo Events

continue using their own configured blind schedules.
