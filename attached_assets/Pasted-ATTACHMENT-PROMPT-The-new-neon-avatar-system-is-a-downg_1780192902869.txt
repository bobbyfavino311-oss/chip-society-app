ATTACHMENT PROMPT:

The new neon avatar system is a downgrade from the original symbol system and needs a complete art-direction pass.

==============================================================
CORE PROBLEM
============

The current avatars feel:

* clip-art
* generic
* mobile game asset pack quality
* overly literal
* cartoonish
* lacking personality
* lacking luxury
* lacking mystery
* lacking nightlife energy

The original symbols had much stronger identity because they felt:

* abstract
* iconic
* premium
* futuristic
* recognizable at a glance

Right now the symbols are trying too hard to literally depict objects.

The result feels tacky.

==============================================================
NEW ART DIRECTION
=================

Think:

Miami nightlife.

Neon signs glowing in the dark.

NOT:

Emoji style.
Sticker style.
Cartoon style.
Clip-art style.
Children's mobile game style.

==============================================================
REDESIGN RULE
=============

Do NOT draw the actual object.

Create an ICONIC SYMBOL inspired by the object.

Examples:

BAD:
A detailed martini glass.

GOOD:
A geometric neon shape that instantly suggests a martini glass.

BAD:
A realistic palm tree.

GOOD:
A stylized neon silhouette made from only a few elegant lines.

BAD:
A cartoon poker chip.

GOOD:
A futuristic casino insignia that resembles a poker chip.
