FINAL DESIGN GOAL

Players should always know:

• What stakes they are playing
• What blinds they are playing
• What buy-in range exists
• Whether they are facing AI or live players
• Whether they are joining a public game or private room

Quick Play should intelligently match bankroll brackets.

Private Tables should replace Ranked mode entirely.

This creates a cleaner poker ecosystem, better table selection, and a more authentic casino experience.
