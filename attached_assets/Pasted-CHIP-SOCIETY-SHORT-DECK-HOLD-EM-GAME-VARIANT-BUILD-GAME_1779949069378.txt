==============================================================
1. SHORT DECK HOLD’EM RULESET
==============================================================

Short Deck Hold’em is mostly the same as traditional Texas Hold’em, with these required differences:

DECK:
Use only 36 cards.

Allowed ranks:
6, 7, 8, 9, 10, J, Q, K, A

Remove from the deck:
2, 3, 4, 5

SUITS:
Keep all four suits:
Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs

TOTAL DECK:
36 cards

==============================================================
2. SHORT DECK HAND RANKING CHANGE
==============================================================

Hand rankings mostly match Texas Hold’em with ONE important change:

FLUSH BEATS FULL HOUSE

Required Short Deck ranking order:

1. Royal Flush
2. Straight Flush
3. Four of a Kind
4. Flush
5. Full House
6. Straight
7. Three of a Kind
8. Two Pair
9. One Pair
10. High Card

IMPORTANT:
In Short Deck mode only, Flush must rank higher than Full House.

Do NOT change traditional Texas Hold’em rankings.
