4. THREE CARD POKER RULES
=========================

Use a standard 52-card deck.

No jokers.

Player vs dealer.

Each side receives 3 cards.

Hand ranking:

Straight Flush

Three of a Kind

Straight

Flush

Pair

High Card

Dealer qualifies with Queen High or Better.

If dealer does not qualify:

Ante wins 1:1.

Play pushes.

Pair Plus still resolves normally.

If dealer qualifies and player wins:

Ante pays 1:1.

Play pays 1:1.

If dealer qualifies and dealer wins:

Ante loses.

Play loses.

If tie:

Push.
