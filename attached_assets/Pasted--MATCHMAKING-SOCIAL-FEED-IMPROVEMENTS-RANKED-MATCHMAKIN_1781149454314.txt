# MATCHMAKING + SOCIAL FEED IMPROVEMENTS

## RANKED MATCHMAKING EXPANSION

Open Ranked Matchmaking across ALL multiplayer game modes.

This includes:

- Texas Hold'em
- Three Card Poker
- Blackjack
- Future Poker Variants
- Future Table Games

Players should no longer be restricted from finding ranked opponents.

--------------------------------------------------

## CHIP BRACKET MATCHMAKING

Players should primarily be matched against opponents with similar chip balances.

The goal is to create fair competition and prevent extremely large stacks from constantly facing new players.

Matchmaking should use chip brackets.

Example:

BEGINNER

0 – 100K

----------------

AMATEUR

100K – 1M

----------------

SEMI PRO

1M – 10M

----------------

HIGH ROLLER

10M – 100M

----------------

ELITE

100M – 1B

----------------

LEGEND

1B+

Players should first search within their own bracket.

If no players are available:

Expand search gradually into neighboring brackets.

--------------------------------------------------

## AI OPPONENT INTEGRATION

If no human opponent is available within a reasonable timeframe:

Automatically fill seats using AI players.

The AI should:

- Use realistic usernames
- Use profile avatars
- Post on the social feed
- Have believable chip balances
- Behave like real players

Players should never sit waiting in matchmaking.

The system should feel active at all times. Matchmaking systems commonly broaden pools or use substitutes when population is low to keep queues moving. (Wikipedia)

--------------------------------------------------

## MATCHMAKING DISPLAY

Show:

Searching For Opponent...

Then:

Opponent Found

or

Filling Remaining Seats...

This makes the process transparent.

--------------------------------------------------

# SOCIAL FEED IMPROVEMENTS

## DELETE POST FEATURE

Players must be able to delete their own feed posts.

Add:

Delete Post

to the post options menu.

Only the original author may delete the post.

When deleted:

Remove immediately from:

- Trending
- Following
- Profile Feed
- Global Feed

--------------------------------------------------

## POST ORDERING FIX

When a player creates a new post:

That post should immediately appear at the top of the feed.

Newest posts should always appear first.

Current behavior should be replaced with:

Most Recent → Oldest

unless another sorting mode is selected.

--------------------------------------------------

## INSTANT FEED UPDATE

When posting:

Do NOT require refresh.

Do NOT require re-entering the feed.

Immediately inject the new post at the top.

Example:

Player creates post.

Feed automatically scrolls to top.

New post appears in position #1.

This should feel similar to modern social platforms.

--------------------------------------------------

## PROFILE FEED

Add:

Posts

tab on player profiles.

Players can view all posts made by that account.

Newest first.

Deleted posts disappear everywhere automatically.

--------------------------------------------------

## POST MANAGEMENT MENU

For the author only:

• Edit Post

• Delete Post

• Share Post

• Pin Post (future feature)

For everyone else:

• Report Post

• Share Post

--------------------------------------------------

## DESIGN GOAL

Ranked matchmaking should always find an opponent quickly by using chip-based brackets and AI fill-ins when necessary.

The social feed should feel alive and modern:

- New posts instantly appear at the top.
- Players can delete their own posts.
- Feed updates occur in real time.
- Profile pages show player post history.
- No refreshes required.
