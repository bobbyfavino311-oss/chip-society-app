PROFILE SCREEN – TOURNAMENT STATS LOCKED STATE REFINEMENT

The current Tournament Stats card is much better, but the large centered lock icon is taking up too much space and makes the card feel empty.

--------------------------------------------------
REMOVE
--------------------------------------------------

Remove the oversized centered lock icon.

Do not use a large badge-style lock.

--------------------------------------------------
REPLACE WITH
--------------------------------------------------

Use a small lock icon positioned in the top-right corner of the Tournament Stats card.

Style:

- Small neon purple lock
- Similar size to a settings icon
- Subtle glow
- Clean and modern
- Non-intrusive

The card should feel informative, not blocked off.

--------------------------------------------------
KEEP
--------------------------------------------------

Keep the headline:

TOURNAMENTS COMING SOON

Keep the supporting text:

Tournament statistics will become available when tournaments launch.

--------------------------------------------------
ADD INFO BUTTON
--------------------------------------------------

Add a small circular "i" information button in the top-left corner of the Tournament Stats card.

Style:

- Small neon outline circle
- Matches app theme
- Same style as other informational icons throughout the app

--------------------------------------------------
INFO POPUP CONTENT
--------------------------------------------------

When tapped, display:

TOURNAMENT STATISTICS

Tournament statistics will begin tracking automatically when tournaments become available.

This section will track:

• Tournament Entries
• Tournament Wins
• Final Table Appearances
• Cash Finishes
• Biggest Prize Won
• Best Finish Position
• Average Finish
• Return on Investment (ROI)
• Tournament Win Rate
• Total Tournament Chips Won

Compete in tournaments to build your tournament record and climb future leaderboards.

--------------------------------------------------
CARD DESIGN
--------------------------------------------------

Keep the existing dimmed statistics visible underneath the overlay.

Reduce opacity to roughly 15–20%.

The user should still be able to see:

Wins
Losses
Win Rate
Best Finish
Biggest Prize

but clearly understand they are inactive.

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

The card should communicate:

✔ Tournaments are planned
✔ Tournament stats already have a dedicated place
✔ Players can see what data will be tracked
✔ The interface feels premium and intentional
✔ The card uses less vertical space
✔ The card feels informative rather than locked or inaccessible

Think:
"Feature Preview"

NOT

"Feature Disabled"
