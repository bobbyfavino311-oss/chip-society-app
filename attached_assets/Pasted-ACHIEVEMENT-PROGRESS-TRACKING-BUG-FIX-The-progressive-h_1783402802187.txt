ACHIEVEMENT PROGRESS TRACKING BUG FIX

The progressive hand achievements are not tracking correctly.

Current issue:

Achievements that require repeated wins, such as:

* Win 10 hands with One Pair
* Win 10 hands with Two Pair
* Win 10 hands with Three of a Kind
* Win 10 hands with a Straight
* Win 10 hands with a Flush
* Win 10 hands with a Full House
* Win 10 hands with Four of a Kind

are staying stuck at:

0 / 10

They should increase every time the player wins a qualifying hand.

⸻

REQUIRED BEHAVIOR

If a player wins a hand with One Pair:

Pocket Rockets should update:

1 / 10

If they win another hand with One Pair:

2 / 10

Continue until:

10 / 10

Then unlock the achievement and award the rewards.

The same logic applies to every other hand-based achievement.

⸻

TRACKING RULE

Only count hands that the player wins.

Do not count hands where the player folds.

Do not count hands where the player loses at showdown.

Do not count hands where the hand type appears on the board but the player does not win.

⸻

ACHIEVEMENT COUNTERS

Each achievement needs its own persistent counter.

Examples:

onePairWins

twoPairWins

threeOfKindWins

straightWins

flushWins

fullHouseWins

fourOfKindWins

straightFlushWins

royalFlushWins

fiveOfKindWins

These counters must be saved server-side.

They must persist after logout, app restart, and device switch.

⸻

HAND RESULT HOOK

After every completed hand, the game should run:

1. Determine winner.
2. Determine winning hand type.
3. If the human player won, increment the matching achievement counter.
4. Save counter to backend.
5. Update achievement progress immediately.
6. If progress reaches requirement, unlock achievement.
7. Award chips / XP / item reward.
8. Show achievement popup.

⸻

REQUIRED EXAMPLES

If the player wins with One Pair:

Pocket Rockets:

+1 progress

If player wins with Two Pair:

Double Down:

+1 progress

If player wins with Three of a Kind:

Set the Table:

+1 progress

If player wins with Straight:

Straight Shooter:

+1 progress

If player wins with Flush:

Suited Up:

+1 progress

If player wins with Full House:

Full House:

+1 progress

If player wins with Four of a Kind:

Quad Squad:

+1 progress

⸻

APPLY TO ALL POKER MODES

This must work in:

* Traditional Hold’em
* Short Deck Hold’em
* Omaha Hold’em
* Joker Hold’em
* AI Practice
* Real Multiplayer
* Private Tables
* Tournaments

Do not only fix one mode.

Use one shared achievement tracking function across all poker variants.

⸻

JOKER HOLD’EM SPECIAL CASE

Joker Hold’em must correctly track:

* One Pair
* Two Pair
* Three of a Kind
* Straight
* Flush
* Full House
* Four of a Kind
* Five of a Kind

If Joker creates the winning hand, it still counts.

Example:

Joker + pair on board = Three of a Kind win.

Increment:

threeOfKindWins

⸻

OMAHA SPECIAL CASE

Omaha must use the final valid Omaha hand.

Exactly 2 hole cards + exactly 3 board cards.

The achievement must count the actual winning Omaha hand, not an invalid Hold’em-style hand.

⸻

SHORT DECK SPECIAL CASE

Short Deck must follow Short Deck hand rankings.

Whatever the winning hand is under Short Deck rules should be the hand type that progresses the achievement.

⸻

UI UPDATE

After each qualifying win:

The achievement page should immediately reflect the new progress.

Example:

0 / 10

becomes

1 / 10

without requiring logout, restart, or refresh.

⸻

ACHIEVEMENT UNLOCK

When progress reaches 10 / 10:

Unlock the achievement.

Award the listed reward.

Example:

Pocket Rockets:

200 chips + 30 XP

Then mark as complete.

Completed achievements should no longer show as locked.

⸻

FINAL VALIDATION

Test:

1. Win with One Pair.
2. Verify Pocket Rockets becomes 1 / 10.
3. Win another One Pair hand.
4. Verify it becomes 2 / 10.
5. Repeat until 10 / 10.
6. Verify achievement unlocks.
7. Verify reward is awarded.
8. Repeat for Two Pair, Trips, Straight, Flush, Full House, Quads.
9. Logout and log back in.
10. Verify progress is still saved.

No achievement requiring repeated winning hands should ever remain stuck at 0 / 10 after qualifying wins.
