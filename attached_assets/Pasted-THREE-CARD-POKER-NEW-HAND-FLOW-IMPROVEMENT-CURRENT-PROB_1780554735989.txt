THREE CARD POKER – NEW HAND FLOW IMPROVEMENT

CURRENT PROBLEM

After a hand finishes, the only available option is:

REBET

This forces the player to immediately repeat the exact same wager amount if they want another hand.

This feels restrictive and creates unnecessary friction.

--------------------------------------------------
NEW REQUIREMENT
--------------------------------------------------

After EVERY completed hand, display TWO buttons:

[ NEW HAND ]
[ REBET ]

--------------------------------------------------
NEW HAND BUTTON
--------------------------------------------------

Purpose:

Start a completely fresh round.

Behavior:

• Clears all previous bets
• Resets Ante
• Resets Pair Plus
• Resets 6 Card Bonus
• Clears Play wager
• Removes previous cards
• Deals no cards until player places new wagers
• Returns table to pre-deal state

Player may choose completely different bet amounts.

Example:

Previous hand:
Ante 100K
Pair Plus 100K
6 Card Bonus 100K

Player taps:

NEW HAND

Result:

Ante = 0
Pair Plus = 0
6 Card Bonus = 0
Play = 0

Table resets.

Player selects any new wager amount.

--------------------------------------------------
REBET BUTTON
--------------------------------------------------

Purpose:

Fast repeat of previous hand.

Behavior:

• Automatically restores previous Ante
• Automatically restores previous Pair Plus
• Automatically restores previous 6 Card Bonus
• Instantly starts next hand

Example:

Previous hand:
Ante 25K
Pair Plus 25K
6 Card Bonus 50K

Player taps:

REBET

Result:

Same exact wagers are restored.

New cards are dealt immediately.

--------------------------------------------------
PLAYER EXPERIENCE GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

Players should never feel forced to repeat the same wager.

Every completed hand should offer:

1. NEW HAND
(Start over)

OR

2. REBET
(Repeat previous wager)

This matches the behavior found in polished casino table games and greatly improves usability.
