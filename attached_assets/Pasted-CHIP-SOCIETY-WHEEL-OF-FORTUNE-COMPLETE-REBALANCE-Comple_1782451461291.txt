CHIP SOCIETY – WHEEL OF FORTUNE COMPLETE REBALANCE

Completely redesign and rebalance the Wheel of Fortune rewards. The current wheel does not properly reflect the in-game economy, and too many cookie variants appear as prizes.

Going forward, the wheel should only award two cookie types:

* Common Fortune Cookie
* Mythic Fortune Cookie

No other cookie rarities should ever appear on the wheel.

⸻

Wheel Design

Keep the original Wheel of Fortune layout.

Revert the wheel back to its original visual style.

Do not use the newer redesign.

Prize labels should be placed diagonally inside each slice, following the angle of the wedge so every prize is easy to read.

Text should always remain inside its slice.

⸻

Prize Icon Standards

Use one consistent visual language.

Chip Rewards

Represent chip prizes only with:

💰 Money Bag

or

A stack of cash icon.

Never use poker chips to represent chip rewards.

⸻

XP Rewards

Represent XP only with:

💎 Diamond

or

⚡ Lightning Bolt

No plain “XP” text by itself.

⸻

Cookie Rewards

Display the actual cookie artwork.

Only two cookie prizes are allowed:

• Common Fortune Cookie

• Mythic Fortune Cookie

Do not include:

* Rare Cookie
* Epic Cookie
* Legendary Cookie
* Theme Cookie
* Holiday Cookie
* Any other cookie rarity

The wheel should never contain more than one Common Cookie slice and one Mythic Cookie slice.

⸻

Visual Improvements

Every slice should display:

* Prize icon
* Prize value
* Diagonal text
* Proper spacing
* No overlap
* Consistent font size
* High readability

The text should follow the angle of the slice and remain centered within its wedge.

⸻

Final Design Goal

The Wheel of Fortune should feel exciting every day while maintaining a healthy in-game economy.

Players should regularly win meaningful chip amounts and XP, while the Common Fortune Cookie provides frequent surprise rewards and the Mythic Fortune Cookie remains an exceptionally rare jackpot prize. Removing all other cookie rarities from the wheel simplifies the reward system, makes the Mythic Cookie feel truly special, and keeps the wheel visually clean and easy to understand.
