Design Goal

The economy should feel rewarding and approachable. A new player should be able to install the game and begin playing poker immediately, while experienced players still have a satisfying progression through increasingly prestigious stake levels. Every blind level, buy-in, AI stack, and table configuration must be internally consistent so that the game’s economy feels polished, fair, and scalable over the long term.
