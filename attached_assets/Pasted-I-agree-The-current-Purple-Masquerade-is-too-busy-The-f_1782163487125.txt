I agree. The current Purple Masquerade is too busy. The feathers, ornate scrollwork, decorative borders, and flourishes are going to be difficult for the AI to render consistently and they’ll compete with the cards and UI.

I’d simplify it dramatically.

⸻

🎭 Purple Masquerade (Minimal Edition)

Theme Goal

Create a sophisticated Venetian masquerade atmosphere using only color, lighting, and a few subtle design cues.

No feathers.

No masks.

No illustrations.

No decorations.

The theme should feel elegant rather than themed.

⸻

Background

Use:

* Deep black-purple gradient
* Very subtle plum glow
* Soft violet haze

No patterns.

No wallpaper textures.

No diamonds.

No line art.

The background should be almost empty.

⸻

Color Palette

Primary:

* Royal Purple
* Dark Plum
* Midnight Black

Accents:

* Soft Gold
* Champagne Gold

Highlights:

* White
* Violet glow

⸻

Background Shapes

Use only:

* Large translucent overlapping circles
* Soft glass-like gradients
* Gentle atmospheric depth

Very similar to the Frozen Neon theme structure.

Just purple instead of cyan.

⸻

Community Card Tray

Design:

* Dark smoked glass
* Thin gold border
* Tiny corner accents
* Soft violet glow

No ornate details.

No flourishes.

No filigree.

No decorations.

Think:
Luxury jewelry box.

⸻

Pot Display

Design:

* Floating black glass capsule
* Thin gold border
* Soft purple glow
* Gold text

Simple.

Premium.

⸻

Lighting

Use:

* Soft purple ambient lighting
* Subtle gold reflections
* Minimal glow

Avoid:

* Bright neon
* Heavy bloom
* Sparkles
* Glitter

⸻

Design Inspiration

Imagine:

* A luxury Venetian hotel suite
* Purple velvet under low lighting
* Gold accents on black marble
* A private high-stakes poker room

Not a masquerade costume.

Not Mardi Gras.

Not fantasy.

⸻

Critical Instruction

The theme should be 90% minimal luxury poker room and only 10% masquerade inspiration.

If someone removed the theme name, it should still look like an expensive premium poker table rather than a decorative themed casino game.
