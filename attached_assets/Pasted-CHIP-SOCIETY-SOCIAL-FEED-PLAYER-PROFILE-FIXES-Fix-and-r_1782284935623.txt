• Load profile from server
• Display username, avatar, level, stats, posts, followers, following
• Do not search again by display name

3. FEED PILLS / TABS REDESIGN

Only use these pills:

• ALL
• TRENDING
• LEADERBOARD
• SEARCH
• ME

Remove all others.

• All newest posts
• Real player posts
• Player’s own posts
• AI-generated filler posts if needed

Newest posts always appear at the top after refresh.

When player creates a post:

Their post immediately appears at the top of ALL.

TRENDING TAB

TRENDING shows:

• Posts with high engagement
• Big wins
• Rare hands
• Fortune Cookie wins
• High-value pots
• Popular AI posts if real content is low

LEADERBOARD TAB

LEADERBOARD shows players with the highest chip balances across the entire platform.

Must update in real time.

Display:

• Rank number
• Avatar
• Username
• Chip balance
• Level

Chip balances must use compact formatting:

1M
500K
1.3B

Do not display values like:

SEARCH TAB

SEARCH allows players to search accounts by username.

Search must return:

• Exact matches
• Partial matches
• Case-insensitive matches

Each result should show:

• Avatar
• Username
• Level
• Chip bracket
• View Profile button
• Follow button
• Message button

Tapping View Profile must open the correct profile.

ME TAB

ME shows the current player’s own social activity.

Display:

• My posts
• My followers
• My following
• My profile shortcut
• My inbox shortcut
• My social stats

Newest own posts appear first.
