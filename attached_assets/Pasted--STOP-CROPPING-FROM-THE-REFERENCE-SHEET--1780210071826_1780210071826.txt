===========================================================
AVATAR PREPARATION PROCESS
===========================================================

For every avatar:

1. Export symbol into its own PNG.

2. Transparent background.

3. 1024x1024 canvas.

4. Symbol centered horizontally.

5. Symbol centered vertically.

6. Equal padding on all sides.

7. No neighboring artwork.

8. No sprite sheet coordinates.

===========================================================
DISPLAY RULES
===========================================================

Avatar image:

object-fit: contain

object-position: center center

No crop offsets.

No sprite sheets.

No coordinate slicing.
