THREE CARD POKER – SHOW WHY THE DEALER WON

CURRENT PROBLEM

The result panel only tells the player:

DEALER QUALIFIES

and then shows wins/losses.

This is not enough information.

Players need to immediately understand WHY they won or lost.

Casino games feel unfair when outcomes are not explained.

--------------------------------------------------
NEW REQUIREMENT
--------------------------------------------------

The result panel must always display:

1. Dealer Hand
2. Player Hand
3. Hand Comparison
4. Reason For Outcome

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

Every hand should answer these questions instantly:

What did I have?

What did the dealer have?

Who had the stronger hand?

Why did I win or lose?

The player should never have to study the cards to understand the result.
