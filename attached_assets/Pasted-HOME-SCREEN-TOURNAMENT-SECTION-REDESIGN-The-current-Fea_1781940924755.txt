HOME SCREEN TOURNAMENT SECTION REDESIGN

The current Featured Tournaments section on the Home Screen needs to be redesigned.

The goal is to keep the Home Screen visually full and professional while avoiding fake tournaments, fake player counts, fake prize pools, fake registrations, or placeholder statistics.

Players should understand that tournaments are a future feature currently under development.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

REMOVE FAKE DATA

Remove all placeholder tournament information.

Examples:

❌ Fake Prize Pools

❌ Fake Seat Counts

❌ Fake Registered Counts

❌ Fake Active Tournament Counts

❌ Fake Progress Bars

❌ Fake Buy-Ins

❌ Fake Timers

❌ Fake Player Numbers

Do NOT remove the section entirely.

The Home Screen feels empty without it.

Instead:

Convert the area into a Tournament Preview Hub.
