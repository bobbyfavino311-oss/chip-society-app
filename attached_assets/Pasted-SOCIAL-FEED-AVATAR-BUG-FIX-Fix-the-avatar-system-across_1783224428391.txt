SOCIAL FEED AVATAR BUG FIX

Fix the avatar system across the entire social feed.

Current bug:

When a player creates a post, the post shows a random avatar instead of the player’s selected avatar.

The selected avatar only appears correctly on the player’s profile, but not anywhere else.

This must be fixed globally.

⸻

REQUIRED BEHAVIOR

The player’s selected avatar must appear consistently everywhere the player is displayed.

This includes:

• Player profile
• Social feed posts
• Home screen Trending Now posts
• Comments
• Replies
• Search results
• Followers list
• Following list
• Inbox conversations
• Notifications
• Leaderboards
• Tournament results
• Multiplayer tables
• Private tables

If the player changes their avatar, all future displays must use the newly selected avatar.

⸻

SOURCE OF TRUTH

The selected avatar must come from one server-side profile field.

Example:

selectedAvatarId

or

activeAvatarId

Do not randomly assign avatars when rendering posts.

Do not use fallback/random avatar unless the player has never selected one.

⸻

POST CREATION FIX

When a player creates a social post:

Save the post with:

• userId
• username
• selectedAvatarId
• timestamp
• post content

The avatar displayed on the post must match the player’s current selected avatar at the time of posting.

⸻

POST RENDERING FIX

When rendering a post:

Fetch avatar from:

post.selectedAvatarId

or from the player’s profile:

user.selectedAvatarId

Do not use randomAvatar()

Do not use defaultAvatar()

Do not use AI avatar assignment logic for real players.

⸻

REAL PLAYER VS AI PLAYER

Separate avatar logic clearly.

Real players:

Use their selected avatar only.

AI players:

May use assigned AI avatars.

Never apply AI avatar randomization to real player posts.

⸻

AVATAR CHANGE BEHAVIOR

When player changes avatar:

Update:

selectedAvatarId

server-side.

Then refresh every place that uses player identity.

Future posts must use the new avatar.

Optional:

Existing posts may either keep the avatar used at posting time or update to the current avatar, but the behavior must be consistent.

Recommended:

Existing posts update to the current selected avatar for simplicity.

⸻

FINAL TEST

Test:

1. Select avatar A.
2. Create a social feed post.
3. Confirm post shows avatar A.
4. Open Home Trending Now.
5. Confirm post shows avatar A.
6. Comment on a post.
7. Confirm comment shows avatar A.
8. Search username.
9. Confirm search result shows avatar A.
10. Change to avatar B.
11. Create another post.
12. Confirm new post shows avatar B.
13. Logout/login.
14. Confirm avatar remains correct everywhere.

No real player post should ever display a random avatar again.
