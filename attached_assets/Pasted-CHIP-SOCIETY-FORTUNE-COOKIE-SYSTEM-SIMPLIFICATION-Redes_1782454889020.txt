CHIP SOCIETY – FORTUNE COOKIE SYSTEM SIMPLIFICATION

Redesign the Fortune Cookie system to remove unnecessary rarity tiers and make the progression cleaner and easier to understand.

The cookie system should now contain only four rarities:

* Common
* Rare
* Epic
* Legendary

The Uncommon cookie has been removed entirely.

The Mythic Cookie remains exclusive to the Daily Wheel and should not appear in the standard Fortune Cookie collection.

⸻

Cookie Collection

Update the collection to display only:

🥠 Common

🥠 Rare

🥠 Epic

🥠 Legendary

Remove every reference to Uncommon cookies throughout the app.

This includes:

* Cookie inventory
* Cookie opening screen
* Cookie counters
* Cookie rewards
* Cookie descriptions
* Achievement rewards
* Drop tables
* Admin tools
* Statistics

Any existing Uncommon Cookies owned by players should automatically convert into Common Fortune Cookies.

⸻

Updated Reward Values

These values should align with the newly balanced game economy and Daily Wheel.

🥠 Common Cookie

Rewards:

• 5,000–25,000 Chips

• Small XP rewards

Description:

“A simple fortune containing small chip rewards and a little extra experience.”

⸻

🥠 Rare Cookie

Rewards:

• 25,000–150,000 Chips

• Medium XP

• Small Ticket rewards (rare)

Description:

“A rarer fortune offering better chip rewards and occasional premium items.”

⸻

🥠 Epic Cookie

Rewards:

• 150,000–750,000 Chips

• Large XP

• Tickets

Description:

“Epic fortunes contain premium rewards for experienced players.”

⸻

🥠 Legendary Cookie

Rewards:

• 750,000–2,000,000 Chips

• 5–25 Tickets

Description:

“Legendary fortunes are exceptionally rare and contain Chip Society’s most valuable standard rewards.”

⸻

Mythic Cookie

Do not display Mythic alongside the standard cookie collection.

Instead, show a small premium badge that reads:

⭐ Daily Wheel Exclusive

Description:

“The Mythic Fortune Cookie can only be won from the Daily Wheel jackpot.”

It should never appear in the normal cookie inventory progression.

⸻

Progression

Players unlock cookies as follows:

* Common — Available immediately.
* Rare — Unlocks at Level 25.
* Epic — Unlocks at Level 50.
* Legendary — Unlocks at Level 100.
* Mythic — Daily Wheel exclusive only.

⸻

Design Goal

The Fortune Cookie system should now feel cleaner, easier to understand, and consistent with the rebalanced game economy. Players progress naturally from Common to Legendary while the Mythic Cookie remains a true jackpot that can only be obtained from the Daily Wheel.
