Complete overhaul of the XP, Level, Prestige, and Rank system.

CURRENT PROBLEM

The current progression system is completely broken.

Players can currently gain hundreds of levels within minutes.

This destroys:

* Long-term retention
* Prestige
* Player status
* Rank significance
* Achievement value
* VIP aspirations

Levels should be one of the most important status symbols in Chip Society.

The progression system should be designed around months and years of gameplay, not hours.

⸻

CORE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

A player’s level should immediately communicate:

* Experience
* Dedication
* Time invested
* Skill
* Social status

Most players should never reach the highest levels.

The highest ranks should feel legendary.

When players encounter a Level 1000+ player, it should feel rare.

When players encounter a Level 2000+ player, it should feel almost mythical.

⸻

REMOVE CURRENT SYSTEM

Completely remove the existing XP formula.

Completely remove the current rank structure.

Remove rank names such as:

* Neon Gold
* Neon Platinum
* Similar generic gaming titles

These feel cheap, generic, and do not match the premium luxury casino atmosphere of Chip Society.

⸻

NEW LEVEL CAP

Increase maximum level cap to:

Level 5000

This creates years of progression.

Very few players should ever reach the upper levels.

⸻
