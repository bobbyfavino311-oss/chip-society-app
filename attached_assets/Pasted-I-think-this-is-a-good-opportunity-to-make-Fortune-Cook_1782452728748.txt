I think this is a good opportunity to make Fortune Cookies into a real progression system instead of just random loot. Right now players collect them, but there isn’t a satisfying sense of “unlocking” higher tiers. Here’s a complete redesign prompt.

⸻

CHIP SOCIETY – FORTUNE COOKIE SYSTEM OVERHAUL

Completely redesign the Fortune Cookie system so it feels like a collectible progression feature rather than simply random rewards.

The UI should remain clean and minimal, but every cookie rarity should have a clear purpose and method of obtaining it.

Cookie Collection UI Improvements

* Show a lock icon over cookie rarities that have not yet been unlocked.
* Display the unlock requirement underneath each locked cookie (e.g., “Unlocks at Level 25”).
* Once unlocked, replace the lock with the player’s owned quantity.
* Keep Mythic visually separate with an “Exclusive” badge labeled Daily Wheel Only.

This gives players a clear long-term progression path while preserving the Mythic Cookie as a true jackpot reward that remains exciting even for high-level players.
